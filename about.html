My Story

Hi, im Synth. Im a year old, amateur and aspiring developer from North East England. I had began my developing journey around November of 2022, making simple python scripts, and in early December I created my first website, quickly followed along by many, many more simple webistes building my skills in HTML and CSS. At this point I decide I wanted to step up my development to the next level, so I turn to javascript. I spend a month or so learning javascript. I was now confident at creating any website from scratch. I then continued learning new skills like, AutoHotKey & Lua. I also became much stronger at my base skill, frontend development.

After this, my first large project was client work for a friend of mine. I was tasked with creating a fully responsive, photography blog website. You can find the finished product at [surf-pics.com].
